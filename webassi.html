Annual Tech & Cultural Fest

GITAM proudly presents the Annual Tech & Cultural Fest where students showcase their talents in cultural activities , Unlimited Buffet, Friction Cosplay,The event features a variety of cultural performances, fun games, music, and dance, showcasing the talents and creativity of students. It also helps freshers build confidence, make new friends, and feel comfortable in their new academic journey..

Event Highlights

The event includes fun games and ice-breaking activities to help freshers interact and build friendships. Special attractions such as the Mr. and Ms. Fresher contest add excitement and encourage participation. A photo booth captures memorable moments, while refreshments and snacks ensure everyone enjoys a pleasant and engaging evening..

Event Schedule

  • Inauguration Ceremony
  • Technical Workshops
  • Cultural Programs
  • Prize Distribution

All students are invited to participate and enjoy the event!
